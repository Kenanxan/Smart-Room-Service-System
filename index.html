System surface

What the private product covers

Guest room menu

QR-based ordering flow for hotel guests with category browsing, item detail views, cart actions, and a checkout path.

Operations board

Task visibility for restaurants or suppliers with status-based work queues, order contents, timing, and action states.

Admin visibility

Revenue summaries, order volume, and hotel or supplier performance insights presented in a management dashboard.
